## Intro
> 🔗 https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graph_algebra

In [mathematics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mathematics "Mathematics"), especially in the fields of [universal algebra](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Universal_algebra "Universal algebra") and [graph theory](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Graph_theory "Graph theory"), a **graph algebra** is a way of giving a [directed graph](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Directed_graph "Directed graph") an [algebraic structure](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Algebraic_structure "Algebraic structure"). It was introduced by McNulty and Shallon, and has seen many uses in the field of universal algebra since then.
