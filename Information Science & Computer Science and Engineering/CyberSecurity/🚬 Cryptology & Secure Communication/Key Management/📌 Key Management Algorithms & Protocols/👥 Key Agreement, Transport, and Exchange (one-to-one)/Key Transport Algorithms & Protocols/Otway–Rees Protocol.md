## Intro
> 🔗 https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Otway%E2%80%93Rees_protocol

The **Otway–Rees protocol** is a [computer network](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Computer_network "Computer network") [authentication](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Authentication "Authentication") [protocol](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Communications_protocol "Communications protocol") designed for use on [insecure networks](https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=Insecure_network&action=edit&redlink=1 "Insecure network (page does not exist)") (e.g. the [Internet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Internet "Internet")). It allows individuals communicating over such a network to prove their identity to each other while also preventing [eavesdropping](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Eavesdropping "Eavesdropping") or [replay attacks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Replay_attack "Replay attack") and allowing for the detection of modification.
